Who are we?

How do we match customer to influencer?

At Foxford Consulting, we employ a variety of proprietary machine learning algorithms to match your business needs to our vast network of influencers. When our algorithm matches your product or idea with an influencer, we reach out to them with an offer.

Benefits to partnering with us include:

  • Live metrics
  • Targeted marketing
  • 100,000+ contacts

Where do we find out influencers?

Since our company has a breadth of influencers in our network, you could find your company represented on YouTube, Instagram, Twitter, or any of the numerous social networks.

Scam prevention

As a largely online company, we understand the potential for scams, both on the consumer and influencer front. As a result, we hold payments until all aspects of the transaction are complete.

Additionally, before any company makes its way to the consumer, we thoroughly vet all companies for their legitimacy and inform the influencer of any risk involved in partnering with the company. This ensures no party is left unknowing during this process.
